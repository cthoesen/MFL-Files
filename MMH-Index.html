MyFantasyLeague.com Franchise Setup - Next Steps
Hide Message

Now that you have successfully logged in, please take some time to customize your own personal information and familiarize yourself with the site:

  1. Verify your franchise information, your mobile setup, your other contact information, and customize your franchise with logos and icons.
  2. Review your league scoring rules, as well as other league settings to make sure you understand exactly how your league is run.
  3. Your league is set up to have an long-running (email) auction. See our Help Center to learn more about how to place an auction bid.
  4. Once rosters are filled, you can use the league calendar to view various events throughout the season , including when you can do "waivers", or adding/dropping players from your roster.
  5. During the season, you will need to submit your lineup each week of the season.
  6. In-season homework regarding who to start, who to add/drop, and more can be done using the Top Performers/Player Stats report, as well as the NFL News Articles report.
  7. Trash-talking is an important part of any fantasy football league, and we offer many ways to help out on that front, available from the menu at the top of this page, including a league message board, league polls and even video trash-talking!

You'll find MyFantasyLeague.com has a ton of features and options, and we encourage you to explore all of the menus above to see all of what we offer, and if you have any questions, contact your league commissioner, or check out our Help Center.
